Crystal Collector!

Instructions:

When the player start the game shown the random number.

When the player clicks on a crystal, it will add a specific amount of points to the player's total score.

In each crystal have a different amount and it reveal once the player click on it, and it will update the score counter.

If the player total score matches the random number from the beginning of the game, the player win. if the player total score is below than the random number, the player lost.

When the player wins or losses the game will restar, once the player play again each crystal will assign new four hidden numbers values, and the score will reset to zero.
